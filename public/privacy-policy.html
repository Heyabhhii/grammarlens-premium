GrammarLens Premium — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026  |  Developer: Antier Solutions

Short version: GrammarLens sends your document text to LanguageTool's public API for grammar checking. It uses Google OAuth to apply fixes via the Google Docs API. We do not store your documents, track your usage, or share data with third parties.

1. What data we process

  • Document text — The text content of your open Google Doc is sent to the LanguageTool public API (api.languagetool.org) for grammar and spelling analysis. Text is transmitted over HTTPS. LanguageTool's own privacy policy governs their handling of submitted text.
  • Google OAuth token — When you sign in with Google, a short-lived access token is obtained via chrome.identity.getAuthToken and stored temporarily in the extension's service worker memory. It is used exclusively to call the Google Docs REST API (docs.googleapis.com) to apply corrections you explicitly request. The token is never transmitted to any server other than Google's APIs.
  • Settings — Your language preference, check interval, highlight colours, and other settings are stored locally in chrome.storage.sync and synced across your Chrome browsers via Google's storage sync service. These settings do not leave your Google account's infrastructure.

2. What data we do NOT collect

  • We do not store, log, or retain document text after a grammar check completes.
  • We do not track which documents you open, how often you use the extension, or what corrections you apply.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking SDKs.
  • We do not share any data with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties.

3. Google OAuth scopes

GrammarLens requests the following OAuth scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/documents — Required to apply text corrections directly to your Google Doc via the Docs REST API.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.email — Used only to display your account email address in the diagnostics panel.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/userinfo.profile — Used only to display your display name in the diagnostics panel.

OAuth access is optional. If you do not sign in, grammar checking and inline suggestions still work; only the "Fix" button (which writes changes to Google Docs via the API) requires OAuth.

4. Third-party services

  • LanguageTool (Privacy Policy) — Document text is sent to their public API for grammar analysis.
  • Google APIs (Privacy Policy) — OAuth tokens and document fix requests are processed by Google's infrastructure.

5. Data retention

No document data is retained by GrammarLens beyond the duration of a single grammar check request. Settings are retained in chrome.storage.sync until you reset or uninstall the extension.

6. Children's privacy

GrammarLens is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
